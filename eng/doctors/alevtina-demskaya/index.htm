Doctor Alevtina DEMSKAYA
  • Graduated from Russian Academy of Sciences Moscow Medical College with a specialty in paramedics.
  • She has furthered her studies in Moscow Institute of Medico-Social Rehabilitation, receiving qualification as a doctor with a specialty in General Medicine.
  • Doctor Demskaya has completed clinical residency at the Institute of Nutrition RAMS with a specialty in Therapy.
  • In 2006 she has completed vocational training in Dietology in the State Medical Academy of Postgraduate Education “Roszdrava”
  • In 2010 she has completed vocational training in The Institute of Continuing Vocational Education of Federal Medical Biological Agency with a specialty in Allergology and Immunology.
  • Teaching in the Department of Postgraduate Professional Education for Premed Graduates in the Medical College №2 since 2012.

Doctor Demskaya has completed many vocational training programs:

  • 2012 – “Therapy” in People’s Friendship University of Russia.

  • 2017 г. - “Therapy” in People’s Friendship University of Russia.
