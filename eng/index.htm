Therapy

We are glad to have in our team highly qualified general practitioners that uses only up-to-date diagnostic and treatment methods.

    Surgery

    Due to constant development and technical progress, the modern surgery has become minimally invasive. Special attention is paid to reduce postoperative period and enhance rehabilitation.

      Gynecology

      Gynecology is a branch of medicine that focuses on women health and the treatment of a wide range of diseases: from simple infections to female genital organs tumors; from plastic surgery of external genital organs to major reparative surgery.

        Urology

        Urology is a branch of medicine that focuses on diagnostics and treatment of genitourinary system diseases, both in men and women (kidneys, urinary bladder and urinary tracts diseases).

          Otorhinolaringology

          Otorhinolaryngology (or often otolaryngology) is a branch of medicine specializing in diagnostic and treatment of ear, throat and nose diseases, as well as head and neck diseases.

            Endoscopy

            Endoscopy – internal organs examination with an endoscope. During endoscopic examination, the endoscope is inserted into the cavities through the natural orifices, for example, into the stomach – through the mouth and esophagus, into the bronchial tubes and lungs – through the larynx, into the bladder – through the urinary tract.

              Ultrasound examination

              Ultrasound examination is based on ultrasonic waves of high frequency. This method has been used for many years for diagnostics in different medical areas.

                Laboratory

                In French Clinic patient can have any test, from general blood test to complex genetic investigations. The procedure room in French Clinic works in a way to provide patient maximum comfort, doing all procedures quickly and safely with single-use supplies.

                  Cosmetology

                  Modern cosmetology uses safe and highly effective methods for skin tightness and plasticity improvement, increasing its oxygenation and correction of vascular abnormalities, for skin rejuvenation and whitening.

                    Phlebology

                    Phlebology is a branch of medicine that focuses on diagnostics, treatment and prevention of varicose vein diseases. It’s a part of angiology – science of blood vessels.

                      Acupuncture (Reflexoterapy)

                      Acupuncture is a branch of traditional Chinese medicine. Acupuncture consists in the influence of special thin needles inserted in biologically active acupuncture points of the human body.
