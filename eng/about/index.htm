Welcome to French Clinic

 

Do you know where to find a good doctor?

This is the question we ask our friends when we feel something wrong with health.

Everyone understands that the success of treatment mainly depends on doctor.

A good doctor wishes to work in a clinic where:

  • He can widely apply the accumulated experience and fully realize his professional potential.
  • He has at his disposal the most modern equipment and instruments.
  • He can give to his patient as much time as necessary.

FRENCHCLINIC is exactly this place

Here:

  • The best conditions have been created for both patients and doctors.
  • We have all the necessary for achieving the main goal – Our patients’ good health

 

Professional team

Team of doctors with a long standing practical experience in French and Russian medical institutions

Modern equipment

The clinic is equipped with all the necessary for successful prevention, diagnosis and treatment, including a modern operating room and in-patient department

Сonvenient location

A cozy, small clinic is located in the heart of Moscow, in a quiet area of the Golden Mile
